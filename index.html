Instagram Clone

InstagramClone is a photo sharing app similar to Instagram but using Parse as its backend.


  • Users can sign up, log in, and securely log out using Parse authentication.
  • Authentication persists across app restarts for the current user.
  • Users can take photos or upload images from their device.
  • Captions can be added, and photos posted to an "Instagram-style" feed.
  • Polished app icon and modern bottom navigation.
  • Feed design mimics Instagram for a professional user experience.
